# Pre-Call Video Script (2–3 minutes)

**Purpose:** Reduce no-shows by removing anxiety, building excitement, and reframing the call as valuable regardless of outcome.

**Tone:** Travis speaking directly to camera. Confident, warm, zero hype. Like a friend telling you what to expect.

---

## SCRIPT

**[OPEN — Direct to camera, casual setting]**

Hey — congratulations on booking your strategy call. I'm Travis, and I want to take two minutes to tell you exactly what's going to happen so you know what to expect.

**[BEAT — slight lean in]**

First, let me tell you what this call is NOT.

It's not a high-pressure sales pitch. It's not someone reading a script at you for 45 minutes. And it's not one of those calls where someone just tries to get your credit card number.

**[PAUSE]**

Here's what it actually is:

You're going to get on the phone with someone from my team who's helped hundreds of people start Amazon businesses. They're going to ask you about your situation — where you are right now, what your goals are, and what's been holding you back.

Then they're going to do something most programs won't do — they're going to give you honest feedback. If Amazon FBA isn't the right fit for you right now, they'll tell you that. And if it is, they'll map out a realistic plan for how you'd get started based on YOUR budget, YOUR schedule, and YOUR experience level.

**[SHIFT — address the doubt directly]**

Now — I know what some of you are thinking. You're thinking, "I'm not sure I'm ready yet." Maybe you don't have a product idea. Maybe you're not sure you have enough money to start. Maybe you tried something before and it didn't work out.

Here's the thing — this call is specifically designed for people who are still figuring things out. You don't need to have all the answers before you get on the phone. That's literally what the call is for.

**[SOCIAL PROOF — quick hit]**

I've had over a thousand students come through our programs. Students like Mina, who's done over $1.8 million in sales. Or Jeremy, who built his entire business while working a full-time job. Every single one of them had doubts before their first call. The ones who showed up are the ones who got results.

**[CLOSE — simple, direct]**

So here's what I'd ask you to do:

One — add this call to your calendar right now so you don't forget. There's a button right below this video.

Two — scroll down this page. I've put together some short videos that answer the most common questions people have before their call. Watch the ones that apply to you — you'll feel a lot more confident going in.

Three — show up. That's it. Just show up, be honest about where you are, and let my team help you figure out the next step.

I'll see you on the other side.

**[END]**

---

## PRODUCTION NOTES

- **Length target:** 2:00–2:30
- **Setting:** Simple, well-lit. Travis's office or a clean background. Nothing fancy.
- **Energy:** Conversational, not presenter-mode. Like a FaceTime call with a friend.
- **No B-roll needed.** Straight to camera the entire time.
- **No music.** Or very subtle, low background music at most.
- **Captions:** Add burned-in captions for mobile viewers (most traffic is mobile from YouTube).
